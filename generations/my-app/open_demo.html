ALPA-4: Interactive Blending Board

Digital Interactivity & Gamification Implementation

Features Implemented:

  • Virtual blending board with drag-and-drop letter tiles
  • Text-to-Speech (TTS) auditory feedback
  • Mobile haptic vibration support
  • Adaptive gamification with difficulty adjustment
  • Multisyllabic 'boss levels' every 5 levels
  • FCRR color-coded activities (Purple/Orange/Green)
  • Accessibility features (large text, high contrast)

Launch Demo:

Open Interactive Demo

Test Instructions:

  1. Click letter tiles or drag them to spelling slots
  2. Click "🔊 Say Word" to hear TTS pronunciation
  3. Fill all slots and click "✓ Check Answer"
  4. Build a streak of 3+ to increase difficulty
  5. Reach level 5 to unlock BOSS LEVEL
  6. Try different FCRR activity levels (Purple/Orange/Green)
  7. Test accessibility features at the bottom

Word Progression:

  • Level 1-3: CVC Words (cat, dog, mat)
  • Level 4-6: Digraphs (ship, chat, fish)
  • Level 7-9: Blends (black, plant, clock)
  • Level 5, 10, 15...: BOSS - Multisyllabic (button, rabbit)
